Follow these steps to start PALM in interactive mode with totalview debugger:
1. $./alloc_script_totalview
2. After the allocation has been granted, $./run_script_totalview
3. In the totalview window, press "play" button.
4. In the totalview window, a dialog box will appear. Press "yes" to stop job and set breakpoints. You may need to nagivate to the desired function in the right-hand pane.
5. In the totalview window, press "play" again to continue.
